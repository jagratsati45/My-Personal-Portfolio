About Me

Hello! I'm Jagrat Sati, a passionate Web Developer and Junior Full Stack Developer pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at Amrapali University. I specialize in building seamless, intelligent digital experiences from the ground up.

My goal is to merge robust backend functionality with intuitive, AI-powered user interfaces. I'm driven by a curiosity for what's possible and a desire to create applications that are not only functional but also a joy to use.
